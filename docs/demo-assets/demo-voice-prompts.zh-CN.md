# Demo Voice Prompts / 演示语音指令

## 场景一：导入 WhatsApp 聊天后生成回复

中文：

```text
帮我分析这个客户想要什么，然后生成一个 WhatsApp 回复。回复要跟着他的聊天语言，语气自然，简短一点。
```

English:

```text
Analyze what this customer wants and draft a WhatsApp reply. Match the customer's chat language, keep it natural and short.
```

## 场景一：保存客户并设置提醒

中文：

```text
把 Omar 存成客户，并提醒我下周一上午 10 点回复他。
```

English:

```text
Save Omar as a lead and remind me next Monday at 10 AM to reply to him.
```

## 场景二：语音口述房源并生成推广链接

中文：

```text
我要推广一套 DHA Phase 5 Lahore 的 10 marla house，4 bedrooms，5 bathrooms，demand 4.35 crore，near park，renovated kitchen，ready possession，适合 family buyer。帮我生成 WhatsApp 和 Facebook 推广文案，并生成 tracking links 和报名链接。
```

English:

```text
I want to promote a 10 marla house in DHA Phase 5 Lahore. It has 4 bedrooms, 5 bathrooms, demand is 4.35 crore, near park, renovated kitchen, ready possession, suitable for a family buyer. Create WhatsApp and Facebook promotion copy, tracking links, and a lead page.
```

## 场景二：查看推广带来的报名

中文：

```text
显示这个推广带来的新客户。
```

English:

```text
Show new leads from this promotion.
```

## 场景二：根据报名回复客户

中文：

```text
给 Sara 生成 WhatsApp 回复，告诉她 owner slightly negotiable，明天下午 5 点可以看房，问她是否方便。回复要简短自然。
```

English:

```text
Draft a WhatsApp reply for Sara. Tell her the owner is slightly negotiable, tomorrow 5 PM viewing is possible, and ask if that time works for her. Keep it short and natural.
```

## 场景二：保存跟进并设置提醒

中文：

```text
保存这次跟进，并提醒我明天下午 4 点半联系 Sara 确认看房。
```

English:

```text
Save this follow-up and remind me tomorrow at 4:30 PM to contact Sara and confirm the viewing.
```

## 房源链接识别兜底话术

中文：

```text
如果这个链接识别不了，就用我补充的这些信息生成推广内容和链接。
```

English:

```text
If this link cannot be read, use the property details I provided to create the promotion copy and links.
```
